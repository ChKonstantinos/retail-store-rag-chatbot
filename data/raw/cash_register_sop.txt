Document Title: Cash Register Standard Operating Procedure

Purpose:
This document describes the correct process for opening, operating, and closing the cash register.

Opening Procedure:
At the beginning of the shift, the cashier must count the starting cash amount.
The amount must match the opening balance recorded in the system.
Any difference must be reported to the shift supervisor before sales begin.

During Sales:
The cashier must scan all products, confirm prices, and issue a receipt for every transaction.
Discounts or manual price changes require supervisor approval.
Cash, card, and voucher payments must be recorded correctly in the POS system.

Refunds at Cash Register:
Refunds must only be processed after the return has been approved according to the Returns and Refunds Policy.
The cashier must attach the original receipt or return approval note to the daily cash report.

Closing Procedure:
At the end of the shift, the cashier must count cash, card receipts, and vouchers.
The closing balance must match the POS report.
Any cash difference must be documented and reported to the store manager.
